About me

I am currently a graduate student at University of Chicago, Department of Statistics and working in Matthew Stephens lab. My primary research area is Statistical genomics, in particular, analyzing RNA-seq data and trying to investigate the cell-type or tissue-type effects from multiple sample single cell/ bulk RNA sequencing.
